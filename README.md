## 👋 Welcome

Welcome to **LibreVault**, the flagship project of **TamilLibre**.

LibreVault is a carefully curated collection of Free & Open Source Software (FOSS), designed to help students, developers, creators, Linux users, and anyone looking for high-quality free software.

Whether you're searching for a Photoshop alternative, a self-hosted cloud, a code editor, or the best Linux tools, LibreVault aims to be your trusted starting point.

Our goal is simple:

> **Help everyone discover the best free software—organized, verified, and easy to find.**

## 🌏 About TamilLibre

TamilLibre is a community focused on promoting:

- 💻 Free & Open Source Software
- 🐧 Linux
- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence
- 🔒 Privacy & Security
- ☁️ Self-Hosting
- 📚 Learning & Education
- 🌍 Digital Freedom

Started by the Tamil community, built for everyone.

## 🎯 Our Mission

To build one of the world's most comprehensive and trusted directories of Free & Open Source Software, making it easy for anyone to discover, compare, and use high-quality software without barriers.
